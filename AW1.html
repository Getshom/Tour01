7 nights tour package

  • AIRPORT / NEGOMBO (Approx. 15 Min / 15 km)

    Ayubowan! Have a wonderful and a memorable Holiday! With this beautiful greeting we welcome you to the island of Sri Lanka! Meet our representative in the arrival lounge at the airport after you clear all customs and immigration formalities.

    Arrive at the Katunayake International Airport. You will be transferred to Negambo. Check in at hotel for overnight stay.

  • NEGOMBO / WILPATTU (Approx. 04 Hours / 160 km)

    After breakfast, visit the fishing village in Negombo. It is fascinating to see the processes and way of life that happens there on a daily basis, afterwards we leave for Wilpattu. Overnight stay at hotel

  • WILPATTU / HABARANA (Approx. 02 ½ Hours / 100 km)

    Morning 06:00 – 09:00 AM safari in Wilpattu National Park with packed breakfast. one of the biggest and oldest of Sri Lankan national parks, home to elephants, sloth bears, mugger crocodiles, leopards and much more. Back to hotel refresh and leave to Habarana. .

  • HABARANA

    After breakfast proceed to Sigiriya to climb the Rock Fortress and go on a village tour and experience a typical Sri Lankan village lifestyle followed by a bullock cart ride into the village and catamaran ride on the lake.

    Sigiriya(5thc.A.D)an impressive rock fortress, a royal citadel of a king for 18 years. Apart from the great rock, the surroundings, just gets you…. so green, so serene, and peaceful. Exciting climb to the summit to the site of the palace which commands a magnificent view of the royal water gardens on par with the best of the ancient world.

    Late afternoon safari at Minneriya National Park to witness the “gathering of elephants”. See over 150 elephants, young and old and babies converge to enjoy the lake with receding water levels to play, socialize, romance and have fun. Thereafter, return back to the hotel; enjoy your overnight stay.
    .

  • HABARANA / TRINCOMALEE (Approx. 02 ½ Hours / 90 km)

    After breakfast we leave for the east coast town of Trincomalee . We arrive at our beach resort with its crystal clear blue waters and white sands. Relax on the beach.Overnight stay.
    .

  • TRINCOMALEE

    Early morning (05 am) pick up from hotel and leave to location for Whale watching tour 3-4 Hours Late afternoon short tour of Trincomalee location of one of the finest harbours in the world, see famous sights such as Fort Fredrick (Portuguese Dutch then British), Koneswaram Hindu temple on the cliff known as Swami Rock, from where a Dutch maiden is believed to have jumped to her death seeing her fickle lover sailing away!
    .

  • TRINCOMALEE / COLOMBO (Approx. 05 ½ Hours / 280 km)

    After breakfast depart to Colombo En-route you can visit Kandy then Proceed to Colombo. Overnight stay.

    "Kandy, The last capital of the Sri Lankan kings which is a world heritage city. The name Kandy conjures visions of splendour and magnificence. Many of the legends, traditions and folklore are still lovingly kept alive. Drive around the Kandy Lake built by the Last Sinhalese king Sri WickramaRajasinghe in 1806, visit the Kandy town and bazaar, the arts and crafts museum and lapidary. The evening is the best time to visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic. Thereafter, witness a traditional cultural show.
    .

  • COLOMBO / AIRPORT (Approx. 30 Mints / 40 km ) one way

    Breakfast at hotel. Short city tour and leave to airport for your flight back home with wonderful memories.
    .
