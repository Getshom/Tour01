8 nights tour package





  • AIRPORT / KANDY (Approx. 04 Hours / 170 km)

    Ayubowan! Have a wonderful and a memorable Holiday! With this beautiful greeting we welcome you to the island of Sri Lanka! Meet our representative in the arrival lounge at the airport after you clear all customs and immigration formalities.

    After you have arrived at the Katunayake International Airport. You will be transferred to Kandy en route visit Pinnawala elephant orphanage. Check in at hotel and Evening visit the Temple of tooth relic and witness a traditional Kandyan dance cultural show. Overnight stay at hotel.

  • KANDY / NUWARA ELIYA (Approx. 04 Hours / 170 km)

    After breakfast visit Royal botanical garden, Kandy city tour and Gem Museum famous Kandy markets. Transfer to Nuwara eliya en route visit Tea estate & tea factory to observe the production process of world famous Ceylon tea and Tea tasting and Ramboda waterfalls. Evening city tour and walk around Gregory Lake Overnight stay at hotel. 

  • NUWARA ELIYA / ELLA (Approx. 04 Hours / 170 km)

    Early Morning visit Horton plains with packed breakfast. Back to hotel and refresh. Afternoon we board our train at NanuOya at 12.30pm for a scenic journey to Ella. This is considered one of the best train journeys in the world for its charm and landscape beauty. En route we will see waterfalls, suspension bridges and tea pickers on our journey as we wind our way along and slowly downwards. Meet our chauffeur guide at Ella station (15:30) and leave for Ella. Overnight stay at hotel. .

  • ELLA

    Early morning leave for Haputale. Near the Dambatenne Tea factory, the Lipton’s Seat lookout rivals the views from World’s End. En route visit Dambatenne Tea factory and learn all about tea, The most magnificent drink. Learn how it is grown, plucked and refined plus the fine art of tea making.

    Evening cooking demonstration at hotel. Chef will teach you how to prepare authentic Sri Lankan dishes.
    .

  • ELLA / YALA (Approx. 02 ½ Hours / 90 km)

    Morning, we trek to little Adam’s peak which gives a panoramic view down to the southern plains of Sri Lanka. After, leave for Udawalawe en route we stop at the mesmerizing Ella Gap to observe the panoramic view of the surrounding area and Rawana falls and proceed to Udawalawe. If time permits we will visit Eth AthuruSevana - Elephant transit home to see baby elephants during their feeding times. Overnight stay at yala. .

  • YALA / MIRISSA (Approx. 03 ½ Hours / 130 km)

    An exciting day dawns, morning 06:00 – 09:00 AM we are on safari at yala national park. We will try our luck to spot the elusive leopard.

    Afternoon leave to Mirissa passing beautiful coastline scenery, the sea lapping the sand and swaying palms. En route visit to the Hummanaya Blow-hole. Second largest blowhole in the world. Evening relax at beach.
    .

  • MIRISSA

    Day at leisure on the beach.
    .

  • MIRISSA / COLOMBO (Approx. 03 ½ Hours / 180 km)

    After Breakfast leave for Colombo. En route Visit Galle Galle is an exquisite city filled with colonial Dutch and Portuguese history and architecture and once a major port. Late afternoon we visit Galle a 17th century Dutch fortified city with a natural harbour exuding old-world charm especially inside the Old Dutch fort – a UNESCO heritage site.

    Galle, famous for its lace, is filled with priceless crafts and antique treasures like jewellery, ceramics, and Dutch period furniture and brass work.Check in at hotel. Colombo city tour and shopping. Evening May be a visit to Galle face green and walk around and street food market. Overnight stay at hotel.
    .

  • COLOMBO / AIRPORT (Approx. 30 Mints / 40 km ) one way

    Breakfast at hotel. Short city tour and leave to airport for your flight back home with wonderful memories. .
